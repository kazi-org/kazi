# E47 -- Fleet observability follow-up: event river + roadmap-ref starmap (P2, ADR-0057/ADR-0056)

Back-link: [docs/plan.md](../plan.md) (master index). Sibling epics: [plans/](.)

E46 shipped the fleet dashboard's approved starmap UX (wave bands, attention
queue, drill-in heatmap, transcript peek; T46.10 live-proven on v1.78.0). Two
pieces of the approved design were explicitly deferred out of it and are this
epic: the **event river** (the Mission Control element absorbed into the
starmap design -- a fleet-wide live feed of loop events, now buildable on the
T46.2 `events.jsonl` sinks) and the **roadmap-ref wiring** (the starmap's
wave-band layout rendering a REAL roadmap goal-file through `GoalSource`
instead of only the seam -- the first user-visible consumer of the ADR-0056
roadmap direction, without yet making the roadmap a first-class read-model
object).

Read-only stance unchanged (ADR-0011 at fleet scope): both surfaces are pure
projections; neither mutates a run, a goal, or a lease.
